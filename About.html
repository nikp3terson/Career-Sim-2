About-

Raised around the beautiful natural landscape of his home state, Maine-
Nik Peterson has always enjoyed the awe and inspiration that can only be
found in nature. While studying at The University of Maine at Farmington
Nik developed a love for film photography and travel. After graduation,
Nik packed his belongings into a van and started to roadtrip America. Five
trips across country later, he decided to settle down in the beautiful state
of Colorado to focus on building a career.

Nik now lives in Colorado Springs with his girlfriend, dog, and embrassingly
large collection of VHS tapes.
